About Suntronic Solar

Suntronic Solar is a leading and most trusted Solar EPC and I&C service provider company founded by professional technocrats from engineering, energy, management and marketing fields committed to providing clean, affordable solar electricity to individuals, businesses, and utilities in order to support the transition to a sustainable global energy supply. Driven by passion for renewable energy, the founders have united their strengths and years of combined expertise to establish a leading provider of cost-effective, cutting-edge, and high-quality renewable energy solutions in India. At Suntronic, we recognise that making a major investment in solar energy for your house can be challenging. We guarantee to work closely with you at every stage of the procedure and we are there to make sure you have a hassle-free experience. In addition, our empanelment with state nodal agencies enables us to provide subsidies on solar systems for residential customers. With a skilled internal design, engineering, and construction team, as well as extensive industry knowledge and technical proficiency, Suntronic is able to create PV solar plants of any size that are the most economical and energy efficient. With our rapid growth, we aim to become a world leader in the solar industry. By using advanced technology from world - class suppliers, we are able to meet international standard in terms of cost leadership, operational scalability and reliability.
